Reach For The Stars


You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four stars below. By clicking on a star you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the target number.

The value of each star is hidden from you until you click on it

Each time the game starts, the game will change the target and the values of each star
