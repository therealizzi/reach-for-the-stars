Reach For The Stars


You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four stars below. By clicking on a stars you will ad a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each star is hidden from you until you click on it

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each star
